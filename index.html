Top 7 Tips for Stress-Free Military Car Shipping

1. Plan Ahead

Start planning your car shipment as soon as you receive your PCS orders. Early planning ensures you have enough time to research options, compare quotes, and schedule your shipment.

2. Take Advantage of Military Discounts

AmeriFreight offers exclusive military discounts to make car shipping more affordable for service members and their families. Be sure to ask about these discounts when booking your shipment.

3. Choose the Right Transport Option

Decide between open auto transport and enclosed auto transport based on your vehicle type and budget. Open transport is cost-effective, while enclosed transport offers extra protection for high-value vehicles.

4. Prepare Your Vehicle

Clean your car, remove personal belongings, and document its condition with photos before shipping. AmeriFreight provides a helpful guide on how to prepare your car for shipping.

5. Be Flexible with Pickup and Delivery Dates

Flexibility with your shipping dates can help you secure better rates and ensure a smoother process. AmeriFreight works with you to find the best schedule for your needs.

6. Stay Informed

AmeriFreight keeps you updated throughout the shipping process. Their team is available to answer questions and provide updates on your vehicle’s status, giving you peace of mind during your PCS move.

7. Work with a Trusted Provider

Choose a reliable car shipping company like AmeriFreight. With years of experience and a commitment to customer satisfaction, AmeriFreight is a trusted partner for military families.
